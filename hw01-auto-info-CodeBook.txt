
This dataset consists of observations describing cars sold in the US in 1978.


The variables in this dataset are arranged in columns

Variable 	Location  	Values
                (columns)   
obs 	        1-3 	 
make 	        5-23 	        name of car make & model
price     	29-33 	        price in 1978 $
mpg 	        42-43 	        fuel efficiency in miles/gallon
rel 	        53-53 	        consumer reports reliability  1=worst up to 5=best
foreign 	56-63 	        text variable="Domestic" for cars made in USA and " Foreign" otherwise
weight   	70-73 	        weight of car in lbs
